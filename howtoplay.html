HOW TO PLAY


object of the game

each participant attempts to beat the dealer by achieving a count as close to 21 as possible (by drawing cards) without going over 21


the game

- the dealer and the player both recieve two cards for the first hand

- the player will is able to see the first two cards, while only seeing one of the dealers cards.

- all face cards (J, Q, K)are worth 10, with the exception of ACE, in which it’s value is equal to 1 or 11

- the player will have the option to HIT or STAND based on their hand.

- if the player gets a total of 21 in their hand, the player wins, and vice versa.


hit

- The player draws a card from the deck and the value of the card is added to the total of the player’s hand

Ex: Player has a 3, and a 5, totalling to 8. Player hits and draws a J. 10 is added to the player’s total, and their total is now 18.


stand

- The player no longer draws any more cards, and their hand total is unchanged.

- The dealer will then reveal both of the cards in their deck.

- If the total of the dealer’s hand is less than 17, they must hit until their total is above 17.


ace

- if the player toal < 11, ace card will equal to 11

- if the player total = or > 11, ace card value will equal to 1


conditions

- if player total = 21, the player wins

- if dealer total = 21, the dealer wins

- if dealer total > 21, dealer busts and player wins

- if player total > 21, player busts and dealer wins
